SendWaste

Blockchain-Powered Waste Management for African Cities

The Problem

Abuja and most African cities face a critical waste management crisis:

  • 60% of residential neighborhoods have no formal waste collection
  • Residents have zero incentive to properly dispose of waste
  • Municipal authorities have no data on waste generation patterns
  • Informal waste pickers operate in exploitative, invisible markets
  • No accountability, no transparency, no trust in the system

The result: Environmental degradation, public health risks, and billions in lost economic value.

Our Solution

SendWaste uses Cardano blockchain to create a transparent, incentivized waste management platform.

How It Works

  • Wallet Connection: Users connect ADA wallets (Eternl, Yoroi, Lace, Typhon)
  • Waste Submission: Submit waste with photo, type, and weight verification
  • On-Chain Verification: Each submission creates immutable blockchain proof
  • Token Rewards: Users earn tokens directly to their wallets
  • Transparent Economics: Leaderboards, recycling markets, and community competition
Key Innovation: We're not just digitizing waste collection. We're creating economic infrastructure that makes waste valuable and waste management transparent.

Why Cardano

  • Low Transaction Costs: Affordable for millions of micro-transactions
  • Environmental Alignment: Proof-of-stake matches our sustainability mission
  • Smart Contract Capability: Aiken enables complex verification logic
  • African Adoption: Growing Cardano ecosystem across the continent
  • Proven Security: Academic-backed blockchain for financial trust
