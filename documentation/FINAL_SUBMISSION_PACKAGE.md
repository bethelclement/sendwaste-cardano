## ðŸ“„ SHORT PROJECT SUMMARY (For Tally Form)

**Problem:**  
Nigeria produces 32 million tons of waste annually, but 70% goes uncollected due to zero trust and accountability in the systemâ€”collectors have no proof of work, citizens can't verify service delivery, and government lacks data for decisions.

**Solution:**  
SendWaste uses Cardano blockchain and Aiken smart contracts to create verifiable proof of waste collection. Users submit waste with photo + GPS, smart contracts verify in <30 seconds, and users receive instant $WASTE tokens and NFT receipts, making invisible environmental labor visible and valuable.

**Why Blockchain/Cardano:**  
Cardano's $0.15 transaction fees (vs Ethereum's $5-50) make micro-transactions economically viable. Aiken smart contracts provide formal verification preventing bugs. 1,000 TPS capacity scales to 100,000+ users. 99% less energy than proof-of-work aligns with environmental mission.

**Target Users:**  
Primary: Informal waste collectors seeking income recognition; Secondary: Residents wanting accountable waste services; Tertiary: Corporations needing verifiable ESG impact data; Government: Municipal waste management transparency.

**Future Plans:**  
Q1 2025: Mainnet launch with 500 Abuja pilot users. Q2: First corporate ESG client. Q3: Scale to 10,000 users across 3 cities. Q4: Government contract secured. Year 2-3: National expansion, 100K users, $2.5M ARR.
